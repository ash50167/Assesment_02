Our History

Foundation and Vision:

In 2010, the company was founded with a vision to revolutionize financial services through technology. With a small team of passionate innovators, the company set out to create user-friendly financial solutions.

2010

First Major Product Launch:

The company's first major product, a mobile payment app, was launched in 2012. It quickly gained popularity for its ease of use and security features, setting a new standard in mobile payments.

2012

Expansion and Growth:

By 2014, the company expanded its services to include international money transfers. Strategic partnerships with global banks and financial institutions fueled rapid growth and customer acquisition

2014

Innovation in Digital Banking:

In 2016, the company introduced its digital banking platform, offering users a seamless online banking experience. This innovation marked a significant milestone, transforming traditional banking methods.

2016

IPO and Global Recognition:

the company went public in 2018, marking its IPO as a major success. The public offering garnered global recognition, attracting investors and solidifying its position in the fintech industry.

2018
